Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 2024

1. Introduction

Arcane Engineering LLC ("Company," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use Arcane Engine ("Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Repository References

We collect and store references to the GitHub repositories in which Arcane Engine is installed.

2.2 Data Generated by Arcane Engine

Any data produced or generated by Arcane Engine during its operation is stored in our database. This data includes, but is not limited to, code, configurations, and any outputs generated by the Service.

2.3 Credentials for Third-Party Integrations

For integrations with third-party tools and services, we store credentials such as API keys. These credentials are encrypted before storage to ensure their security.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Internal Use

The information we collect is used solely for the purpose of providing, maintaining, and improving the Service. We do not share this information with any third parties.

3.2 No Tracking or Usage Data Collection

We do not track user activity within the Service, nor do we collect any usage data.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage

All data is stored on our infrastructure hosted by Google Cloud. We utilize industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, breaches, or other security issues.

4.2 Data Retention

Currently, we do not have a data retention policy in place, meaning all data is stored indefinitely. We may update our data retention practices in the future.

4.3 Encryption

Credentials for third-party integrations are encrypted before being stored to ensure their security.

5. User Control and Data Rights

5.1 Access and Deletion

You have full control over your data. If you wish to access or delete your account and all associated data, please contact our support team at support@arcane.engineer.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or change this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice. Your continued use of the Service after any modifications to the Privacy Policy will constitute your acknowledgment of the modifications and your consent to abide and be bound by the modified policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at support@arcane.engineer.
