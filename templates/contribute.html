Contributing

Build new tools and automations

The entire suite is free and open-source. We welcome contributions from the community. If you're interested in building a tool like this for your own team, check out our Development Kit and CLI.

To give you an idea of what the engine can do for you, here are some examples from our Lab:

  • Read Helm chart and build scripts to generate docker-compose file
  • Break down a large module into smaller ones in seconds
  • Scrape project to generate beautiful README file

If you have any questions or need help, feel free to reach out to us at support@arcane.engineer.
